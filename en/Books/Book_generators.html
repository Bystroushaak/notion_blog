Book generators

Last update @2020/01/28

Over the years, I've felt many times the need to create an ebook from something that wasn't meant to be ebook, or maybe it was released only in HTML format, instead of an epub.

This page contains list of all such generators.

Meaningness.com epub generator

Convert meaningness.com to nice and relatively clean epub.

https://github.com/Bystroushaak/meaningness.com_epub_generator/

Environment and programming language Self

This is a script that takes a bunch of .html files from my 📂Series about Self and converts them to epub.

https://github.com/Bystroushaak/Environment_and_programming_language_self_epub_generator

Artificial Intelligence epub

Epub generator for the Artificial Intelligence - foundations of computational agents book.

This was my first attempt to create an ebook from something.

https://github.com/Bystroushaak/Artificial-Intelligence-epub
