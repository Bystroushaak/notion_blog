Weekly updates

Regular weekly updates, eh? Inspired by the post Staying Motivated by Sending Status Updates to Nobody and by Eevee's dev log, I've decided to try to write regular weekly updates, where I'll put together what I've done, experienced and generally survived through.

I am not sure how much this will be interesting to other people, as this should be a tool for myself, to remember what I've done.

PS: I live in the country where week starts with Monday.
